Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Adobo paste
  • olive oil
  • Onion
  • Garlic Cloves (3)
  • Salt & Pepper and Other Spices

Steps

  1. Blend all marinade ingredients until smooth
  2. Marinade chicken for 1 hour
  3. Grill/Saute chicken until cooked
  4. Put it all together and hulk smash the food
