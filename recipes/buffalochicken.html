Buffalo Chicken Sandwich

Big time sandwich I have been making since I was 20 years old. That's over a decade. Damn, I am getting old.

My wife defintely makes fun of me for this one too. I haven't made it in a while though because its unhealthy.Super tasty, but can't eat this stuff on a caloric restriction, that's for sure.

Defintely the tastiest buffalo chicken sandwich known to man. I can't speak highly enough of this meal. Shoutout to Steak Escape for showing me the ropes on this one, and for not getting too mad when I didn't show up for work every now and again...

Ingredients

  • Frozen Chicken Tenders
  • Franks' Red Hot Sauce
  • Butter
  • Shredded Cheddar Cheese
  • Ranch Dressing

Steps

  1. Cook tenders in oven
  2. Mix butter and Franks in a sauce pan
  3. Cut chicken and put it in the pan with sauce
  4. Put it all together and hulk smash the food
